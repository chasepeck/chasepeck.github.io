A crappy game made with crappy Scratch
The Skiller was a character that me and my friends came up with. I was bored one day so I took that character and made it into a game.
I made it in Scratch because I didn't want to put the time in to making a GameMaker Studio 2 game if I was just going to make it for fun.
I wanted to publish it, so I'll upload the .sb3 file. You're going to need Scratch Desktop v3 or a Scratch account to play this game.
